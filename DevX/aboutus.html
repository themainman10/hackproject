How to sign up

Here is how to get started with DevX, with 4 simple steps. step 1: make an account with an email and password. Step 2: upload a picture of your prescription. Step three: enter your address and payment info. Step 4: get instant saving and consistent prices on prescription medications like insulin. Step 5 (optional but highly recommended) connect with your insurance company to help subsidize the cost of the prescription.

What happens next

We here a DevX will consistently and quickly deliver your medication based on your prescriptions refill frequency. For example once a month or once a week, whichever fits your needs and works best for you.

Price

The price of your prescription will always be consistent and rarely change. We have contracts with major drug makers to ensure drugs will be sold at a fixed price for a long duration of time so the price you pay will always remain consistent.

How you save money when you use DevX

P5 DevX is able to sell mediation that is vastly cheaper than a pharmacy without sacrificing the quality of medication, for two reasons. One we buy wholesale from drug makers directly. Second, we cut out the middlemen. Oftentimes the middle men are directly responsible for the high fluctuations of the price for a medication, and for the high cost of a medication.
